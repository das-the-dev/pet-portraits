Put your portrait photos in THIS folder.

For example, save a file here called:
    bella.jpg

Then open  src/lib/portfolio.ts  and add  image: "/portfolio/bella.jpg"
to that pet's entry. (Note: the path starts with /portfolio — you do NOT
include the word "public".)

Tips for good photos of your artwork:
  • Shoot in bright, even daylight (near a window is perfect).
  • Fill the frame with the artwork, straight-on, no glare.
  • JPG or PNG both work great.
